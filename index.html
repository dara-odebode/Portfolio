Hi, I’m Dara.

I'm a 2nd year Computer Science student at the University of Surrey. With my diploma in Fashion Design and Technology, and a certificate in Graphic Communications Technology, I'm interested in the creative part of tech, specifically in software development and UI/UX design. I create web projects both independently and collaboratively, and I'm passionate about making tech more accessible and fun.

I currently work as a social media content creator & events assistant for my university. I'm also a web developement intern at Lark Park Theatre

My latest acheivement was being awarded for the Santander Open Academy Scholarship, and in June, I won 1st place in the Food Waste Hackathon hosted by Climate Jams and the University of London. My team’s idea, ScrapSavers, is a zero-food waste recipe app that turns food scraps into snacks.

When I’m not making websites, I spend time reading books mostly by Tracy Deonn and Emily Henry, rewatching La La Land, sketching, scrapbooking, cooking, and improving my graphic design skills.
