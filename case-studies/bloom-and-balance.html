Bloom and Balance Case Study

Designing a website from scratch to help women better understand their menstrual cycle, reducing period pain, balancing hormones, working with their current energy levels and improving their wellness overall.

01

Problem Statement
When I am on my device looking for information on the menstrual cycle, I want all the advice and tips on one platform, so I can save time and learn how to best care for myself during each phase.

02

Obectives & Goals

  • Have all the helpful advice and information on each phase of the menstrual cycle in one website.
  • Educate women on their menstrual cycle to improve overall wellness and work with their current energy level.

03

Research
I looked through the large collection of posts I had saved on pinterest over the years, as well as websites including Healthline, Cleveland Clinic and WebMD to have more professional, reliable information.

06

User Needs

  • User needs information of the luteal, menstrual, ovulatory and follicular phase in one platform.
  • User wants to find out more about her menstrual cycle.
  • User wants to learn how to work with her energy levels during each phase.

07

Features & Functionalities

To solve the problem, the user needs...

  1. 1. An accessible, easy-to-navigate website where they can find all the information on their menstrual cycle.
  2. 2. A carousel where they can choose whcich phase of the cycle to learn more about.
  3. 3. A single page for each phase so users can view all the categories (diet, movement, self-care, herbs, lifestyle) in one place.
