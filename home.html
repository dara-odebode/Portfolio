Hi, I’m Dara.

I'm a 1st year Computer Science student at the University of Surrey. With my diploma in Fashion Design and Technology and a certificate in Graphic Communications Technology, I'm drawn to the creative part of tech, specifically in front-end web development and UI/UX design. I create web projects both independently and collaboratively and I'm passionate about making tech accessible and fun.

I currently work as a social media content creator for my university, creating, scripting and editing videos to inform and inspire students, especially in STEM fields. This also includes working with different school faculties, especially the faculty of Engineering and Physical Sciences, to raise awareness of events and encourage prospective students to pursue a future degree in computing and engineering.

My latest achievement is my team’s app idea, ScrapSavers, a zero-food waste recipe app that turns food scraps into snacks. Presented at the Food Waste Hackathon hosted by Climate Jams and the University of London, our app won 1st place. I plan to fully develop the app by the end of summer.

I grew up in Aberdeen, Scotland but recently moved to London, England.

When I’m not coding in Visual Studio Code, I spend time reading books mostly by Tracy Deonn and Emily Henry, learning Portuguese, rewatching La La Land, sketching, scrapbooking, cooking, and honing my graphic design skills.
